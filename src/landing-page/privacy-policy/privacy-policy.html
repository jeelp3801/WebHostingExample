Privacy Policy

Who We Are

At Umi, we provide a task management application integrated with Notion and Spotify to help users organize their tasks and enhance their productivity with music.

Information We Collect

  • User Authentication Tokens: Required for secure access to Notion and Spotify.
  • Task Data: Includes task names, steps, and associated dates for task management.
  • Basic Workspace Details: If applicable, to enable features within the app.
  • Music Preferences: Includes information about your playback preferences or currently playing tracks via Spotify.

How We Use the Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable task management features using Notion, such as saving, retrieving, and displaying tasks.
  • To play and display music tracks from Spotify to enhance the user experience.
  • To personalize your app experience based on your task and music usage patterns.

Third-Party Integrations

Notion API

We use the Notion API to access and manage your tasks. This may involve sharing authentication tokens and task-related data with Notion's servers to enable features like saving and retrieving tasks.

Spotify API

We use the Spotify API to provide music playback functionality. This may involve sharing authentication tokens and music-related preferences with Spotify's servers to retrieve playlists, play songs, or display your current track.

Note: Both integrations comply with their respective privacy policies:
